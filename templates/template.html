Info About the Project

This is a sentence generator based off of the Markov Chain algorithm. By parsing a large corpus of text and generating histograms of words, we are able to predict what word might come next based on probabilities. Currently, this is generating a sentence between 15 and 20 words in length with a varying 1st to 4th order Markov Chain from a text of about 700,000 words.
